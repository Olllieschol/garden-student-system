How it works

Protection in three steps

1

You type normally

Write your message in ChatGPT, Claude or any AI tool as you usually would.

2

GuardAI rewrites it before it sends

We automatically replace sensitive data with realistic fake details — a different name, a different number, a different address. The AI receives the fake version and never sees your real information.

3

You get the real answer back

When the AI responds using the fake details, GuardAI automatically swaps everything back to your real data before you read it. You see the real answer. The AI never knew.

Rather stay in control? You can also: Warn only — just get alerted and decide yourself | Edit masks — choose exactly what gets replaced | Send anyway — skip protection for that message
