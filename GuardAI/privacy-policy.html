GuardAI Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026 · Plain English, no legalese.

The short version: GuardAI runs entirely on your own computer. It does not have a server. It does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your data with anyone — including us. There is nowhere for your data to go, because GuardAI never makes a single network request.

What GuardAI does

GuardAI is a browser extension that watches the message box on supported AI chat websites (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini and Microsoft Copilot). Before you send a message it checks the text for sensitive information such as phone numbers, emails, passwords, credit cards, Medicare/passport/TFN numbers, addresses, names and confidential business data. It can warn you, and optionally swap that data for realistic fake data so the AI never sees the real thing.

What data we collect

None. We do not operate any servers and we receive nothing from you. There are no analytics, no telemetry, no crash reports, no accounts, and no tracking of any kind.

Where your data lives

  • On your device only. All detection runs locally in your browser using built-in pattern matching (and, optionally, a locally-bundled AI model that you add yourself). Your message text is never uploaded anywhere by GuardAI.
  • The masking table. When you use masking mode, GuardAI stores a small lookup table that links your real data to its fake stand-in. This is saved using chrome.storage.local, which lives only on this device. It is not synced to your Google or Microsoft account and is never sent off the device.
  • Session statistics. Counts shown on the dashboard (items detected, items masked, platforms used) are also stored only in chrome.storage.local and reset when your browser restarts.

What gets sent to the AI

Only the message you actually submit. If masking mode is on, GuardAI replaces your sensitive data with fake values before the message leaves the page, so the AI provider only ever receives the fake version. When the AI replies, GuardAI swaps the fake values back to your real ones locally, so you read your real information but the AI never knew it.

Note: the AI provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Microsoft) still receives whatever you choose to send and is governed by their own privacy policies. GuardAI's job is to help you avoid over-sharing.

The optional local AI model

GuardAI can use a small Transformers.js model for smarter, context-aware detection. If you enable it, the model files are bundled inside the extension and run on your machine. GuardAI explicitly disables remote model downloading, so even this feature never contacts the internet.

Your controls

  • Turn GuardAI on or off entirely from the dashboard toggle.
  • Turn masking mode on or off at any time.
  • Clear the entire local mapping table with one click from the dashboard — this permanently deletes the real-to-fake links from your device.
  • Uninstall the extension to remove everything it ever stored.

Permissions we request

  • storage — to save your settings, the masking table and session stats locally on your device.
  • Host access to the four supported AI sites — so the content script can read the message box and scan it locally before you send.

We do not request broad browsing access, history, tabs of other sites, or any network permissions.

Contact

GuardAI is a local-only tool with no backend. Because nothing is collected, there is no personal data for us to access, correct, or delete on your behalf — you are always in full control on your own device.
